General Farming Tips

Irrigation Tips

  • Water early morning or late evening
  • Use drip irrigation for water conservation
  • Mulch soil to retain moisture
  • Avoid overhead watering

Fertilization

  • Test soil before applying fertilizers
  • Use organic compost regularly
  • Balance NPK ratios for specific crops
  • Apply fertilizers during active growth

Pest Control

  • Use integrated pest management
  • Plant pest-repelling companion crops
  • Regular monitoring is essential
  • Encourage beneficial insects
